Best Fall & Winter Fragrances for 2026

Updated March 2026 · Cold weather picks for men & women
Fall & Winter Fragrances

Cold weather calls for warm, rich, enveloping fragrances. The light citrus colognes of summer won't cut it when temperatures drop. Here are the best cold weather fragrances across every price range.

Best Winter Colognes for Men

Tom Ford Tobacco Vanille

Tom Ford Tobacco Vanille

Price Range: $175 - $280
Notes: Tobacco Leaf, Vanilla, Cacao

The king of winter fragrances. Rich, sweet, smoky, and incredibly cozy. One spray fills a room. It's expensive, but there's a reason it's been the go-to cold weather scent for over a decade.

Spicebomb Extreme

Viktor & Rolf Spicebomb Extreme

Price Range: $80 - $120
Notes: Tobacco, Vanilla, Lavender

A warm, tobacco-vanilla bomb that's both sweet and spicy. Better performance than the original Spicebomb with 10+ hour longevity. One of the best value winter fragrances.

Parfums de Marly Layton

Parfums de Marly Layton

Price Range: $230 - $300
Notes: Apple, Vanilla, Cardamom

A sweet, warm apple-vanilla scent that's become a cult favorite. Projects like crazy in cold weather and lasts 12+ hours. If you can afford it, Layton is a winter staple.

Azzaro Wanted By Night

Azzaro Wanted By Night

Price Range: $38 - $50
Notes: Cinnamon, Tobacco, Cedar

The budget king of winter colognes. Warm, sweet, and spicy with cinnamon that pops in cold air. At under $50, there's no better value for a cold weather fragrance.

Best Winter Perfumes for Women

YSL Black Opium

YSL Black Opium

Price Range: $80 - $98
Notes: Coffee, Vanilla, White Flowers

The ultimate cold weather perfume. Coffee and vanilla create a warm, addictive base that's perfect for winter nights. A consistent compliment-getter that never goes out of style.

By The Fireplace

Maison Margiela By The Fireplace

Price Range: $100 - $135
Notes: Smoky Wood, Chestnut, Vanilla

Smells exactly like sitting next to a crackling fireplace. Cozy, smoky, and unique. A unisex fragrance that works beautifully on everyone during the coldest months.
