FragranceX vs FragranceNet

FragranceX and FragranceNet are the two biggest discount fragrance retailers online. Both sell authentic products at 30-60% off retail. But which one is better? Here's a detailed comparison.

Pricing

Prices are remarkably similar between the two sites — usually within $1-3 of each other on the same products. FragranceNet runs more promotional coupon codes (often 30-35% off), while FragranceX sometimes has lower list prices before coupons.

Best approach: Check both on SpritzFind before buying. The cheaper option varies by product.

Selection

FragranceNet: Over 17,000 products including fragrances, skincare, aromatherapy, and candles. Slightly broader catalog.

FragranceX: Over 10,000 fragrances. More focused on fragrance specifically. Both carry all major designer and niche brands.

Shipping

FragranceNet: Free US shipping on orders over $59. Standard delivery in 5-7 business days.

FragranceX: Free US shipping on all orders. Standard delivery in 4-7 business days.

FragranceX wins here with free shipping on all orders and no minimum.

Authenticity

Both are authorized retailers that source from legitimate distributors. Products are 100% genuine. The main difference from department store purchases: products may arrive without cellophane, in tester boxes, or in plain packaging. The fragrance inside is identical.

Customer Service & Returns

Both offer 30-day return policies for unused products. Customer service reviews are mixed for both — this is common with high-volume discount retailers. Most complaints relate to shipping times rather than product quality.

The Verdict

Choose FragranceNet if: You want the broadest selection and like using coupon codes for extra savings.

Choose FragranceX if: You want free shipping on every order and slightly faster delivery.

Best approach: Compare both every time you buy. Prices shift weekly.
