How to Find the Best Cologne Deals

1. Compare across retailers. A cologne listed at $50 on Amazon might be $35 on FragranceNet or $32 on FragranceX. Use SpritzFind to compare instantly.

2. Buy the larger size. The price per ml drops significantly on 3.4oz and 6.7oz bottles. A 1oz bottle might cost $30, but a 3.4oz bottle costs $45 — three times the fragrance for 50% more money.

3. Check eBay for authentic deals. eBay's authenticity guarantee covers fragrances over $35. You can find brand new sealed bottles from authorized sellers at 40-60% off retail.

4. Look for gift sets. Around holidays, many brands offer cologne + aftershave + shower gel sets for the price of just the cologne alone. These sets often show up on discount sites year-round.
