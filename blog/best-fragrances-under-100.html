Where to Find Designer Fragrances Under $100

1. FragranceNet & FragranceX — typically 30-60% off retail. Both are authorized retailers with authentic products.

2. Amazon — competitive prices, especially during Prime Day and Black Friday. Stick to "Ships from and sold by Amazon" for authenticity.

3. eBay (authenticity verified) — eBay's authenticity guarantee covers fragrances over $35. Great for finding sealed bottles at wholesale prices.

4. Ulta & Sephora sales — both run seasonal 20% off sales that bring designer fragrances under $100.
